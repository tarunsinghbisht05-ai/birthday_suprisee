Tribute to A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was one of India's greatest scientists and the 11th President of India. He is known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contribution to India's defense and space programs.

He inspired millions of young people with his vision, humility, and dedication to education. His life teaches us to dream big and work hard to achieve those dreams.

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
